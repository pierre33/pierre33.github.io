Pierre Lassalle

I am currently preparing a Ph.D. at the Centre National d'Études Spatiales (French Space Agency) in Toulouse in France where I am performing research on the processing of large remote sensing images. My research focuses mainly on the scalability of segmentation and classification algorithms for large multitemporal series of high resolution images.
The goal of this site is to share my research on large scale remote sensing algorithms and introduce to you some of the libraries I have been developing so far. These libraries are in progress and the presence of some terrible bugs are possible. That is why if you have a question or if you find one of these bugs, please feel free to contact me.
