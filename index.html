Pierre Lassalle

Pierre Lassalle received both his Engineering Degree in Computer Science (ENSTA Bretagne) and Master Degree (University of the Basque Country in Spain) in Computer Science and Intelligent Systems in 2012. He is currently preparing a Ph.D. at the CESBIO laboratory in Toulouse in France where he is performing research on the processing of large remote sensing images. His research focuses mainly on the scalability of segmentation and classification algorithms for large multitemporal series of high resolution images.
