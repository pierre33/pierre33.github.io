Algo Planet

Passionated by algorithmic and programming, I decided to create a section where I can share my thoughts on some algorithmic case studies. The objective will be for each problem to minimize the running time and the space complexity. I really think that the funniest part is the investigation of the most elegant solution. Of course I do not pretend to find the best solution but I just want to share my reasonning and hence, I really invite you to send me a message by proposing me your solution. After checking, it will be a pleasure to mention it in this site with your name or even better your website or github.
I hope you will have fun reading it !
